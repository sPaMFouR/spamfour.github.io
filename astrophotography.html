California Nebula to Pleiades Open Cluster

EXIF: 258 * (1 min, ISO 1600, 135 mm, f/4), Total Integration Time : 4 hr 18 mins
Software: Deep Sky Stacker V4.2.6, Adobe Photoshop CC 2019 and Pixinsight 1.8.9
